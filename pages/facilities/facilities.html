EQUIPMENTS









ACIC MIET Meerut Foundation provide FAB-LAB to the innovators where they can fabricate their product part. This lab is equipped with CNC Milling Machine, Fiber laser engraving machine, CO2 Laser Cutting Machine, All Mechanical Tools, Ultimate Tensile Test Machine, Pillar Drill Machine, Jig Saw Machine, H/V Sander Machine, 3D Printer and 3D Scanner etc.

ACIC MIET Meerut Foundation provide the maker ‘s space to innovators where they can implement their idea. This space equipped with air conditioning and all tinkering tools like soldering station, unlimited internet Wi-Fi, separate cubical and one meeting room with smart board to brainstorm their ideas.
